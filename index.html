      • JURONG LAKE GARDENS

      Jurong Lake Gardens is Singapore’s new national gardens in the heartlands. It is a people’s garden, where spaces will be landscaped and created for families and the community to come together. The 90-hectare Gardens comprises Lakeside Garden (Jurong Lake Gardens West/former Jurong Lake Park), Chinese and Japanese Gardens (Jurong Lake Gardens Central) and Garden Promenade (Jurong Lake Gardens East).Lakeside Garden is open for visitors from end-April 2019. The rest of the Gardens is currently undergoing re-development.

Key attractions


1. Clusia Clove

A 2-hectare water playground modelled after tidal patterns

Parents can bring their children here to engage in water play activities within a more natural environment


2. Forest Ramble

A playground inspired by the natural environment

Multiple areas such as a multiple-storey high slide and fun maze;

are great place to spend time over the weekend or holidays


3. Gardenhouse

The epicentre of all gardening related activities

Patrons can visit the community garden and test out their green fingers


4. Rasau Walk

A meandering 300m boardwalk by the lakeside

AFamilies can take their morning walks and strolls here


5. Neram Streams

Streams that channel stormwater runoff to prevent flooding

This is a great area where children can learn about geography


6. PassionWaVe @ JLG

Numerous water sports activities such as canoeing and kayaking

This is one of the few areas in the west to partake in such activities


7. ActiveSG Park

Houses a gym and swimming pool for exercise enthusiasts

A great place tot workout with a relaxing view of the park
